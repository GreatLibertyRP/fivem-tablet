FAQ


I'm new here. What do I do?
If you just spawned in, try using the Car Rental. You will find this in Leigon Square. On the map, it will be a purple car blip. You can rent a vehicle from him to get around. He will give you Rental Papers, so if you get pulled over then you have proof that the vehicle is yours. It might help for you to go directly to the DMV and get your license.


I got my License, Now what do I do?
We have many Civilian jobs out there. Making money should be easy. See a vehicle in the vehicleshop thats got your eye? Don't have enought money for it? You can finance it, if that's not what you're looking for then Doing the various jobs or other money making activites around the city will give you enough money to get that vehicle. Make sure you return that Rental Vehicle though.

/911ems "message" - Dispatch EMS to a location. "message" - Helpful information for EMS to know.

/report "message" - Sends a message to Staff Only. "message" - IMPORTANT information to include to let Staff know what the issue is.

Taxi Job - Information

To get Job Title: - To start the taxi job, you must have the job title. You can get this 1 of 2 ways. Either go to the City Hall and apply there, or open your phone and open the job center app. Once open, hover over the taxi job line and click the checkmark. Once this is done, you now have the job title of Taxi Driver.

After Gettting Job Title: - Once you have the job title, You will drive to the taxi job center and in the parking lot, you will find a marker to pull out a taxi car. Once you do that you will spawn in a taxi.

After Getting Taxi: - Once you are in the taxi you can roam around the city. If anyone needs a pickup they can do "/taxi" with a message that'll tell you their location and you can pick up other players. In-between those times, you can pick up the locals around the city by opening the radial menu.

Opeing Radial Menu: - To pick up locals around the city, you open your radialmenu. To do this follow these steps: 1. Press and hold F1. 2. Open the "Work" tab. 3. Click the "NPC MISSION" Tab.

Doing NPC Mission: - Once you start a NPC Mission, a marker will be placed on your map with a location to pick up a local. Go to that location. The Meter will automatically start running.

Doing People Missions: - If you are taxing around a person whose flown into the city, Once you pick them up, you will open the radialmenu and go to the work tab and click the "Start/Stop Meter" Tab. This will start the meter. Once you get to their drop off location, you will stop it.
